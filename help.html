The Science of Panic Score

Our unique algorithm calculates your "Panic Score" to prioritize your day. It's designed to balance the objective urgency of a deadline with the subjective difficulty and importance of the task.

Score = (Difficulty [1-5] × Importance [1-5] × Remaining Workload [Hours]) / Hours Left

*Remaining Workload = (100% - Completion %) × Estimated Total Hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Panic Meter work?
The meter represents the weighted average of all your active deadlines. If it turns RED, our system recommends immediate action or delegation.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All your data is stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage. We don't send any task details to external servers.
Can I use this for team projects?
Currently, this is a personal productivity tool. However, you can export your task data as JSON to share with others.
